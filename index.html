The Parisian Experience

Rue du Four is located just steps from many of the finest boutiques, cafes, restaurants, and art galleries Paris has to offer. This exquisite Left Bank apartment offers the perfect setting for an unforgettable stay in the City of Light.

Situated in the charming Saint-Germain-des-Prés neighborhood, known for its literary history, art galleries, and vibrant café culture, Rue du Four places you at the heart of Parisian culture. Within walking distance, you'll find the Luxembourg Gardens, the Seine River, Notre-Dame Cathedral, and many other iconic Paris landmarks.

This beautifully renovated 3-bedroom apartment combines classic Parisian charm with modern amenities. The spacious living area features high ceilings, large windows, and elegant furnishings, creating a comfortable and stylish environment for your stay.

The fully equipped kitchen allows you to prepare meals with fresh ingredients from local markets, while the comfortable bedrooms provide a peaceful retreat after a day of exploring the city. Original Haussmanian features like ornate fireplaces blend seamlessly with contemporary design, making this apartment a true Parisian gem.
